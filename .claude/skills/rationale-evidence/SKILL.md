# Rationale And Evidence

Reference: `src/core/rationale.ts`, `src/core/fixtures/evidenceCards.ts`.

## Cards (max three bullets each)

why measure first; mechanism route; evidence anchors; failure modes; required
controls; claim boundary.

## Evidence relations

supports, requires, assumes, contradicts, confounded_by, falsified_by,
caps_claim_at.

## Rules

Tie every reason to a public anchor or a labeled demo assumption. Use "could" /
"hypothesis". Never use "validated" except to state something is NOT validated.
