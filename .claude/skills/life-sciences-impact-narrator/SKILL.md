# Life Sciences Impact Narrator

## Audience

PI running optical / ODMR / magnetic-sensitive protein measurements — not ML judges.

## Rewrite rules

- Lead with **what to measure first** and **why controls matter**
- Use "public construct hypothesis" not "sensor"
- Mention falsification: "we would abandon this route if…"
- One paragraph max per section

## Template

> Your team asked for [objective summary]. Discover ranked [scaffold] first because
> [route + evidence anchor]. Before any claim of field coupling, run [controls].
> If [falsification bullet], stop — the route failed triage.

## Output

PI email draft (≤200 words) + bullet handoff for grad student.
