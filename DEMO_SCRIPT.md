# Nebula Discover — 3-Minute Demo Script

**Built with Claude: Life Sciences hackathon**

**Opening (0:00–0:20) — the problem**

> "Protein sensor teams can generate ideas faster than they can measure them.
> Every mechanism route needs expensive optical, magnetic, or ODMR measurement.
> Nebula Discover shows what is worth measuring first — and what would falsify it."

On screen: masthead with honesty badges.

**0:20–0:45 — objective compiler (§01)**

Paste the demo objective. Click **Run Discover**.

> "A messy sensing objective becomes structured constraints."

**0:45–1:10 — hypotheses + ranking (§02)**

> "Public construct hypotheses ranked by measurement-worthiness — not predicted
> performance. Weights are open on screen."

Click the top card (LOV/flavin).

**1:10–1:35 — mechanism route + simulation (§03–04)**

> "Transparent causal chain. Synthetic multimodal traces — every plot labeled
> synthetic assumption sweep, not prediction. Parameter space table shows
> transparent assumptions."

**1:35–2:00 — rationale + falsification (§05)**

> "Six rationale cards plus explicit falsification kill rules — what experiment
> would make us abandon this route. Evidence anchors link to real DOIs."

**2:00–2:20 — claim firewall (§06)**

Click **Attempt unsafe claim**, then paste a custom overclaim.

> "The firewall blocks validation language while plots stay visible."

**2:20–2:35 — handoff export (§07)**

Select rank-2 hypothesis, click **Download**.

> "Export follows UI selection — controls, confounders, falsification, swarm
> manifest — ready for a measurement collaborator."

**2:35–2:50 — mandatory swarm (§08)**

> "Every run passes through a mandatory hierarchical map-reduce swarm — 4 sentry
> lenses, 6 committee lenses, severity-weighted consensus, verification
> fingerprints. Claude mirrors this in `.claude/agents/swarm-orchestrator.md`."

Optional: click **Load stress-test objective** to show warnings + arbiter patches.

**2:50–3:00 — close (§09 + footer)**

> "Pipeline, not a model wrapper. Deterministic code owns the engine; Claude is
> a visible panel of agents and skills. Nebula Discover does not claim the sensor
> works — it shows what is worth measuring first, and why."

**Closing line:**

> "Decide what deserves measurement first."
